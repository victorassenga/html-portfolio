Urban Brew

Premium brand identity for a modern coffee experiences.

Overview

Urban Brew needed a modern visual identity that positioned the brand as a premium urban café experience.

Challenge

The brand lacked a clear identity and needed a refined visual system that would work across packaging, interiors and digital media.

Strategy

A minimal modern and clean design that uses strong typography, high contrast colors and timeless visual language.

Icon early visual exploration.

Final Identity

A refined brand system combining modern typography with premium and minimal visual applications.

Brand identity applied to packaging.

Stationery and brand materials.

Typography and color pallete.

Social media brand presence.

Results

The new identity positioned Urban Brew as a premium café brand and significantly improved its visual consistency and recognition.
